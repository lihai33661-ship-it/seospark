### Title: I spent 3 months figuring out Google SEO for my SaaS. Here's what actually worked (and what didn't)

I launched SEO Spark 3 weeks ago. Before that, I spent 3 months learning SEO the hard way — reading Google docs, testing stuff that didn't work, wasting money on tools I didn't need.

If you're a small business owner or solo founder trying to rank on Google, here's the short version of what I learned. No fluff. No "in today's digital landscape."

**What I thought mattered (but doesn't):**

- Writing 3000-word posts. Doesn't help. Google cares about usefulness, not word count.
- Buying backlinks. Got burned twice. One "guest post" service sent me links from a network of 50 sites that all had the same IP. My traffic actually went down.
- Publishing every day. Burned out in 2 weeks. Quality tanked. Nothing ranked.

**What actually moved the needle:**

1. **Picking the RIGHT keywords.** Not "high volume" keywords. Keywords where I could actually compete. For SEO Spark, I target things like "free seo blog writer" and "ai content for small business" — not "best seo tool." The first two have less search volume. But I can actually rank for them, and the people searching are ready to try something.

2. **Writing like a human.** I'm not a native English speaker. My first 10 posts sounded like a textbook wrote them. I deleted 5 of them and rewrote them the way I actually talk. Traffic to those rewrites went up 3x in 2 weeks. Google knows. Readers know.

3. **Actually answering the question.** Most blog posts dance around the topic for 500 words before getting to the point. I stopped doing that. First sentence = the answer. Everything after that = proof. My bounce rate dropped from 85% to 60%.

4. **One specific EEAT trick that works.** I started putting real numbers in every post. Not "studies show" — "I tested this with 12 beta users and 8 of them said X." It's harder to write. But readers trust it. And Google seems to trust it too.

**What I'm still figuring out:**

Forum posting. Reddit. Quora. I've had 4 posts removed by auto-mods because they sounded too "promotional." Still learning the right way to do this.

But the blog part? That's working. Happy to share more if anyone's interested.

## 区别在哪

| 维度 | 原版 | 重写版 |
|------|------|--------|
| 口吻 | SEO顾问教你怎么做 | 一个创业者说"我试过了" |
| 例子 | "For a plumber..."（假设的） | "我花了500刀买外链，流量反而降了"（真的） |
| 结构 | 工整列表 | 有起伏：我以为→实际→还在摸索 |
| 缺点 | 没有 | 我说了自己英语非母语、帖子被删 |
| 可信度 | 中等 | 高（暴露缺点=可信） |
| 论坛存活率 | 低（像广告） | 中高（像真实分享） |

## 关键原则

论坛/社交媒体能活的内容，和博客能排名的内容，**是两套写法**。

- 博客要结构完整、关键词铺排、EEAT信号
- 论坛要真实感、不完美、有故事、有互动钩子
- 共同点：都需要**具体的东西**，不是泛泛的道理

**我两套都能写。问题不是能力，是你决定要我先产哪套。**
