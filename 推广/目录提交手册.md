# SEO Spark 目录提交手册

## 通用资料（复制粘贴用）

**产品名称：** SEO Spark

**网址：** https://seospark.net

**一句话介绍（Tagline）：**
AI blog posts that rank on Google — one keyword, publish-ready in 60 seconds.

**详细描述（Description）：**
SEO Spark generates SEO-optimized blog posts in 60 seconds. Type one keyword and get a complete article with built-in keyword strategy, EEAT signals, meta tags, heading hierarchy, and real-time SEO scoring (0-100). ChatGPT writes words — SEO Spark writes content that ranks on Google.

Use cases: small business SEO, content marketing, blog writing, organic traffic growth. Free tier includes 3 articles — no credit card required.

**分类：** SEO, AI Writing Assistant, Content Marketing, SaaS

**价格：** Freemium — Free (3 articles), Pro $9/month, Lifetime $79

**Twitter：** @gorgeous4ew

**Logo：** https://seospark.net/icon.svg
